# Credit-Risk-XGBoost-ROI-Optimization
Predictive credit risk modeling using XGBoost on a dataset of 255k+ loans, achieving a simulated net value addition of R60.9M through optimized default detection.

📌 The Mission
In the lending world, the difference between a thriving portfolio and a financial loss comes down to one thing: predictive precision. Using a dataset of 255,347 loan applications, I built an XGBoost-powered engine designed to help financial institutions differentiate between high-potential borrowers and high-risk defaults.

💡 The "Why" (Business Impact)
Data science is only as good as the value it creates. By simulating a data-driven rejection strategy for the top 10% highest-risk applicants, this model demonstrates a Net Value Add of R60.9 Million. This isn't just about "accuracy", it's about protecting the bank's capital and ensuring sustainable lending.

🛠️ The Technical "Engine"
I chose XGBoost for its ability to handle complex, non-linear relationships across 18 borrower variables (Income, Credit Score, DTI, etc.).

Calibration is Key: I didn't just look at the final prediction; I validated the model across 10 risk deciles.

The Result: A near-perfect correlation between the model's "predicted risk" and "actual defaults," ensuring that the bank can trust these numbers for real-world decision-making.
